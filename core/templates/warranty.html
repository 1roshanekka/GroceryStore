Introduction

Welcome to the Grocery App Warranty Information. This page outlines the warranty terms for items purchased from our app.

Warranty Coverage

All items purchased from our app come with a standard [number]-day warranty from the date of purchase. This warranty covers manufacturing defects and faulty workmanship.

Exclusions

The warranty does not cover the following:

  • Damage caused by accident, misuse, or neglect.
  • Normal wear and tear of the item.
  • Items with removed or altered serial numbers.

Claim Process

If you believe your item is covered by the warranty, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support team at 1roshanekka@gmail.com to initiate a warranty claim.
  2. Provide the order number and details of the item along with a description of the issue.
  3. Our team will guide you through the rest of the warranty claim process.
